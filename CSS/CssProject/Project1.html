Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk Powder
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Ghee
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom

Recipe

  1. Mix the milk powder, flour and baking powder in a bowl
  2. Separately whisk together the melted ghee, egg and cream
  3. Pour into the dry ingredients and use a spoon to bring together into a dough
  4. Lightly knead the dough for 1-2 minutes to make sure there are no lumps and refrigerate for 20 min
  5. Knead the dough again when it’s cooled to make sure it is smooth. Once it is smooth and binds together properly, stop kneading. If it feels too dry, add a tablespoon of milk and knead
  6. Heat 2" of oil in a wok on medium heat
  7. Serve and enjoy!

Plain Barfi

Ingredients

  • Khoya or Milk Powder
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom or saffron

Recipe

  1. firstly, in a bowl take 2½ cup milk powder, ¾ cup sugar and 1 cup milk.
  2. whisk and mix well until everything is well combined.
  3. transfer the mixture into large kadai. recommend using nonstick pan to prevent from sticking
  4. also add ¼ cup ghee and mix well on low flame.
  5. finally, milk burfi recipe tastes great for a week when stored in an airtight container.
